Javea New Build Villas — Price Guide

Javea (Xabia) occupies a privileged position on the northern Costa Blanca, sheltered by the Montgo mountain and flanked by rocky coves, sandy beaches, and a protected marine reserve. It has long attracted a discerning international community, particularly British, Dutch, and German buyers seeking a quieter, more upscale alternative to the resort towns further south. The new-build villa market in Javea reflects this positioning: entry-level modern villas on modest plots start from around €450,000, while architect-designed homes with infinity pools and panoramic sea views regularly exceed €1,000,000.

In the mid-range bracket of €500,000-€750,000, buyers can expect three to four bedrooms, a private pool, a plot of 500-800 square metres, and contemporary open-plan design with large sliding glass doors. Popular build areas include the Tosalet hillside, Cap Marti, and the Granadella zone, each offering different view orientations and proximity to amenities. Construction quality in Javea tends to be a step above mass-market coastal developments, with many projects involving bespoke architectural firms rather than large-scale developers.
