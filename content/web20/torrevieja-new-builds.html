Torrevieja New Builds for Sale

Torrevieja sits at the heart of the southern Costa Blanca and has evolved from a traditional fishing town into one of Spain's most popular destinations for international property buyers. Its appeal lies in a combination of affordable pricing, reliable climate, and excellent connectivity: Alicante-Elche airport is just 40 minutes away, and the AP-7 motorway links the town to Murcia and beyond. New-build developments in and around Torrevieja range from compact two-bedroom apartments near the salt lakes to spacious bungalows and townhouses in surrounding urbanisations like Los Balcones and Aguas Nuevas.

Prices for new-build apartments in Torrevieja start from approximately €130,000 for a two-bedroom unit with communal pool access, rising to €200,000-€250,000 for larger three-bedroom penthouses with solarium terraces. Many current projects feature energy-rated construction to A or B standard, aerothermal hot water systems, and pre-installed air conditioning. The local rental market is strong, driven by a large Scandinavian, British, and Belgian resident community that sustains year-round demand beyond the summer tourist peak.
