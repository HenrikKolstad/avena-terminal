New Builds in Costa Blanca Under €200,000

Costa Blanca remains one of the most accessible regions in Spain for buyers seeking modern, new-build properties without stretching past the €200,000 mark. Towns like Torrevieja, Gran Alacant, and Guardamar del Segura regularly list two-bedroom apartments and ground-floor bungalows starting from around €140,000, often including communal pools, parking, and energy-efficient features mandated by current building codes. These developments typically sit within a short drive of the coast, and many benefit from the mild southern Costa Blanca microclimate that sees over 300 days of sunshine per year.

At the upper end of this budget you can find three-bedroom townhouses in areas like San Miguel de Salinas and Ciudad Quesada, where newer urbanisations offer generous terraces and proximity to golf resorts. Buyers should pay attention to the building stage: off-plan purchases often come with lower entry prices and flexible payment schedules, while key-ready homes allow you to inspect finishes before committing. Either way, a thorough review of the developer's track record and the community fees is essential. Financing is straightforward for both EU and non-EU residents, with Spanish banks typically offering 60-70% loan-to-value for non-residents.
