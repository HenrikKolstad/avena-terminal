Orihuela Costa New Developments

Orihuela Costa is a sprawling coastal municipality in southern Alicante province that encompasses some of the most popular residential areas for international buyers, including Villamartin, La Zenia, Playa Flamenca, Cabo Roig, and Campoamor. Its appeal is practical: established commercial infrastructure centred around Zenia Boulevard shopping centre, multiple blue-flag beaches, three golf courses, and straightforward access to both Alicante and Murcia airports within 30-50 minutes. New developments here cater predominantly to Northern European retirees and second-home buyers, with a growing segment of remote workers choosing the area for its connectivity and low cost of living.

Current new-build projects in Orihuela Costa range from compact two-bedroom apartments starting at €150,000 to three-bedroom detached villas reaching €350,000 or more. Mid-range townhouses and ground-floor bungalows with private gardens occupy the popular €180,000-€240,000 bracket. Developments in the Villamartin and Lomas de Cabo Roig areas tend to be well-established with mature landscaping, while newer urbanisations closer to Campoamor and Mil Palmeras offer more contemporary architectural styles.
