# $AVY — The Avena Experiment
## Full Strategy Document

### What is this?
The Avena Experiment is a community property raise built on top of 
the Avena Terminal scoring engine. 180 investors. One property. 
One outcome.

### The Concept
The Core — a visual fundraising mechanism on the Avena crypto page — 
fills with USDT from investors. When it hits €450,000 the raise locks. 
Avena deploys into the single highest scored key-ready property in 
Spain at that moment. Yield flows monthly in USDT back to all holders.

### Token: $AVY (Avena Yield Token)
- Network: BSC (Binance Smart Chain)
- Standard: BEP-20
- Total supply: 450,000 (1 token = €1 invested)
- Minimum buy-in: €2,500
- Maximum spots: 180
- Non-transferable during lock period
- Burned on redemption

### The Raise Mechanics
- Hard cap: €450,000 USDT
- Minimum buy-in: €2,500 USDT
- Window: 90 days to fill The Core
- If not filled: 100% refunded automatically via smart contract
- If filled: capital locks, Avena deploys within 30 days

### The Smart Contract
- Network: BSC
- Escrow pattern — funds never touch Avena wallet until raise completes
- Multisig: 2/3 signers required to move funds
- Timelock: 48 hour delay on any fund movement
- Immutable — no upgradeable proxy
- Audit required before launch

### Property Selection
- Avena engine filters all key-ready properties in database
- Highest overall score at raise close date wins
- Criteria: value score, yield score, location score, quality score, risk score
- No human override — algorithm decides
- Property revealed publicly when Core locks

### Timeline
Month 1-3   → The Core open. 90 days to fill.
Month 3-4   → Core locks. Property identified and purchased.
Month 4-5   → Keys received. Property furnished and listed.
Month 5-6   → First tenants. Rental income begins.
Month 6+    → Monthly USDT yield distributed to all holders.
Month 12    → First quarterly exit window opens.

### Yield Distribution
- Target gross yield: 6-8%
- Estimated net yield after all costs: 4-5.5%
- Costs include: property management (15-20%), IBI tax, community fees, insurance, maintenance reserve
- Rental income collected monthly
- Costs deducted, remaining converted to USDT
- Distributed pro-rata to all $AVY holders
- Paid directly to connected wallet

### Exit Mechanics
- Quarterly exit windows from month 12
- Submit redemption request during window
- USDT returned within 30 days
- $AVY tokens burned on redemption
- No secondary market trading during lock period
