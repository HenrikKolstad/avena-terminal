Benidorm New Build Apartments

Benidorm is unique on the Spanish coast: a genuine year-round resort city with a permanent population of over 70,000 that swells dramatically during tourist season. This sustained demand makes it one of the most reliable rental markets on the Mediterranean. New-build apartment developments in Benidorm are predominantly high-rise or mid-rise, reflecting the city's vertical character and the premium placed on sea views. Prices for new two-bedroom apartments start from around €180,000 in the Poniente beach area and the inland Rincon de Loix neighbourhood, while front-line Levante beach addresses and upper-floor penthouses command €300,000 or more.

What sets Benidorm apart for investors is occupancy depth. The city attracts British and Scandinavian tourists from October through April, Spanish domestic visitors during summer, and a growing segment of digital nomads and long-stay retirees year-round. This means rental void periods are shorter than in quieter coastal towns. New developments increasingly target this market with hotel-style amenity packages: concierge services, rooftop pools, co-working spaces, and managed rental programmes built into the community structure. Buyers should verify whether short-term rental licences are available for specific buildings, as Benidorm's local regulations have evolved. Benidorm also benefits from excellent transport links, with Alicante airport 45 minutes away, a TRAM light rail connection to Alicante city, and the Terra Mitica theme park and Sierra Helada natural park nearby.
